Abbreviation key

  • M - Mach number
  • AR - Aspect ratio (ratio between longest and shortest dimension)
  • TR - Temperature ratio (ratio between stagnation temperature at nozzle exit vs ambient temperature of room/atmosphere)
  • NPR - Nozzle Pressure Ratio (Ratio between stagnation pressure vs ambient pressure at nozzle exit
  • Rect - Supersonic rectangular nozzle
  • Diam - Supersonic diamond nozzle
